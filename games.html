Games

One of my favorite franchises is "Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney".

It's a visual novel where you play as a lawyer, and as of now the series spans 6 games with two spin-off franchises, "Ace Attorney Investigations" and "The Great Ace Attorney".

The games in order by release date:

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney (2001)

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney- Justice For All (2002)

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney- Trials & Tribulations (2004)

Apollo Justice: Ace Attorney (2007)

Miles Edgeworth: Ace Attorney Investigations (2009)

Miles Edgeworth: Ace Attorney Investigations- Prosecutor's Path (2011)

An interesting tidbit of trivia, this is the only game within the franchise to not have an official English translation for Western audiences! However, there is an unofficial fan translation available via a patch applied to the official game. You need a DS emulator to play it, however.

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney- Dual Destinies (2013)

Phoenix Wright: Ace Attorney- Spirit of Justice (2016)

The Great Ace Attorney Chronicles (2021)
